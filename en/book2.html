“Find something you would be willing to die for – and when you find it, live for it.”

What does it take to achieve the impossible, to overcome all obstacles and even our own expectations? Or to fulfill our biggest dreams and turn them into reality? The truth is, we are capable of far more than we think. Steven Kotler is one of the world’s best-selling authors in the field of peak performance. He is the director of Flow Research, an organization dedicated to understanding peak performance, and he applies these insights to coaching individuals and organizations on how to reach their highest potential. In this book, Kotler reveals the secrets behind the extraordinary performances of top athletes, artists, scientists, and leaders who have redefined what is possible. Drawing on more than 20 years of neuroscience research, he shows how to push beyond limits and achieve goals that once seemed unattainable. Achieve the Impossible is an inspiring read about unlocking your full potential. If you aim for the stars, don’t hesitate to dive in – no spacesuit required.

“They say there’s no reward without hard work. And if you want to be the best, that rule applies without exception. If you want to achieve something meaningful, you must devote every free moment, every single day, to turning that goal into reality.”
